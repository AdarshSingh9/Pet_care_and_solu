Adoption Tips

Adopting a pet is a big responsibility. Here are some tips to ensure you're ready:

  • Ensure your home is pet-friendly and safe.
  • Consider your budget for food, toys, and vet visits.
  • Make time for regular walks, play, and care.
  • Research the breed or type of pet you're interested in adopting.
  • Patience is key, especially when welcoming a rescue animal.
